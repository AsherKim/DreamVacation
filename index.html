    • Bora Bora

    Bora Bora is an island east of the Fiji Islands. Bora Bora has hut hotels that are on the ocean and has a very tropical feel in them. The Bora Bora Resort has hotels made from Four Seasons and you have a bunch of things you can do like scuba diving, swimming, or just eat and sleep. I want to go to Bora Bora because the hotel prices are 1,448 dollars a night, which means it has to be good.
